Dedicated Servers

Mishtel enhances its cloud telephony offerings with dedicated server solutions for data center applications. Our virtual dedicated data center services, encompassing bare metal, managed dedicated or shared servers, managed firewalls, and virtual desktop infrastructure, alleviate the need for physical server management. Enjoy transparent pricing models, unlike public cloud providers, ensuring cost predictability as your business scales, while leveraging a resilient virtual infrastructure to meet evolving compute demands.

Shared Cloud

Mishtel supplements its cloud telephony services with shared server solutions designed to meet client requirements for data center usage. Shared cloud storage entails storing data from multiple customers on a shared infrastructure, ensuring privacy while maximizing resource utilization. This virtualized service is separate from the underlying hardware, with each customer's data stored in a distinct logical pool, ensuring privacy while facilitating distribution across various servers and locations.

Internet Connectivity

Mishtel offers internet connectivity solutions alongside its cloud telephony services, tailored to suit data center requirements. Whether you seek broadband connectivity, hosted telephony, or dedicated internet access, our solutions are customized to your business needs. Supported by a team of experts and strong partnerships with leading connectivity providers, we provide a diverse range of options, from ADSL and FTTC to dedicated ethernet circuits and 4G failover. Additionally, we offer fully managed MPLS network services for clients operating across multiple sites, ensuring a dependable Wide Area Network (WAN) experience.

Some FAQ’s

Read Most Frequent Questions

  • How does Mishtel ensure the reliability and security of its data center hardware?
    Mishtel ensures the reliability and security of its data center hardware through a combination of stringent measures. These include regular maintenance schedules, proactive monitoring systems, and redundant hardware components to minimize the risk of failure. Additionally, Mishtel employs industry-leading security protocols, such as encryption, firewalls, and access controls, to safeguard data stored on its hardware.
  • What measures does Mishtel take to prevent downtime and ensure uninterrupted service for its dedicated servers?
    To prevent downtime and ensure uninterrupted service for its dedicated servers, Mishtel implements a robust infrastructure with redundancy built into every layer. This includes redundant power supplies, network connections, and storage systems to mitigate the impact of hardware failures. Moreover, Mishtel's expert technicians continuously monitor server performance and promptly address any issues to minimize downtime.
  • Can you explain the software solutions offered by Mishtel for data center management and optimization?
    Mishtel's software solutions for data center management and optimization encompass a range of functionalities aimed at maximizing efficiency and performance. This includes software for server virtualization, resource allocation, and workload management. Additionally, Mishtel offers monitoring and analytics tools to provide insights into system performance and identify areas for improvement.
  • How does Mishtel address connectivity issues for clients with multiple sites using its MPLS network services?
    To address connectivity issues for clients with multiple sites, Mishtel leverages its MPLS network services. MPLS allows for the creation of private and secure connections between geographically dispersed locations, ensuring reliable communication and data transfer. Mishtel's expert engineers configure and manage MPLS networks to optimize performance and minimize latency across all sites.
  • What options does Mishtel offer for backup or failover internet connectivity to prevent disruptions?
    To mitigate the risk of connectivity disruptions, Mishtel offers backup and failover internet connectivity solutions. This includes redundant internet connections from multiple providers and automatic failover mechanisms that seamlessly switch traffic to secondary connections in the event of primary link failures. By implementing redundant connectivity solutions, Mishtel ensures continuous access to the internet and minimizes the impact of network outages on its clients' operations.
